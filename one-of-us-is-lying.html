"One of Us is lying" by Karen M. McManus is a murder mystery! This story is wonderful for someone who is dreaming about solving a mystery, they can help the main characters figure out the details of Simon's death. This book has surprises until the end. The structure of this book is unlike any other book I have read. There are the POV of every character, so you can learn more about theses character. to help ou figure out if they were capable of murder... On the other each time they end one of the characters POV's usually leave you on a cliff-hanger. Happy Reading! See you next time!
