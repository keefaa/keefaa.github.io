I am back with another book to review for you guys! "The Keeper of the Lost Cities" series by Shannon Messenger, is a series of 8.5 books, with a 9th book coming out! The main plot of all the KOLTC books is about a 12 year old girl named Sophie who is a powerful elf but was raised by humans, her past holds a lot of secrets of which Sophie and her friends need to uncover. Uncovering the secrets of her past can help the elvin world demolish the criminals who are on the loose. Happy Reading! See you next time!
